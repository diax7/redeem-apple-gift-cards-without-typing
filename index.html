Redeem Apple Gift Cards Without Typing

Generate scannable codes you can redeem with your iPhone or iPad camera.

Enter 16-character codes. Spaces and dashes are fine — we clean them up.

How to use it

  1. Paste one or more Apple gift card codes above and press Generate.
  2. On your iPhone or iPad, open the App Store, tap your profile picture, then Redeem Gift Card or Code.
  3. Choose Use Camera and point it at a scannable box on this screen, about 4–7 inches (10–18 cm) away.
  4. The code is recognized instantly — no typing.
